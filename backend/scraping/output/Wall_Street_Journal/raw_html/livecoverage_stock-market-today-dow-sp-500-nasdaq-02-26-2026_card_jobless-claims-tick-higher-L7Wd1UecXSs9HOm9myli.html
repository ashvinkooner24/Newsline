Weekly Jobless Claims Rise Less Than Expected

By

Jessica Coacci

,

Reporter

212,000

That's the number of people who filed for unemployment benefits in the week through Feb. 21.

It is up from 208,000 a week earlier, but below the 215,000 claims that economists surveyed by The Wall Street Journal expected.

U.S. Jobless Claims Rose Last Week

Continuing claims, which scale with the total size of the unemployed population, fell.

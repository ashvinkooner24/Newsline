Best-selling deals worth shopping this week

From DeWalt tools to Apple AirTags, these are the items our readers can’t get enough of.

Beckham Hotel Collection bed pillows (48% off)

Get a plush pillow at a budget-friendly price with this Beckham Hotel Collection set. A soft cover and feathery fill work best for side sleepers who prefer a cushioned feel. 

DeWalt jig saw (45% off)

Make all different types of cuts with a DeWalt jig saw. The bright LED light helps when working in low-light conditions, and the long-running battery saves you from getting caught up in a cord.

Mini chainsaw (36% off)

A mini chainsaw chops up fallen branches and breaks up brush in seconds. Just use one hand, and rely on its battery power so there’s no need for an extension cord.

Apple AirTag 4-pack (35% off)

Add an AirTag to your most important valuables and never lose them again. The FindMy network makes tracking lost items a breeze.

Ultra pain relief cooling neck pillow (33% off)

This ergonomic pillow cradles your neck without flattening out, giving you the support many traditional pillows don’t. It’s tilted at a 15-degree angle to mimic the natural curve of your neck.

Apple AirPods 4 with A.N.C. (13% off)

Apple’s AirPods 4 deliver Active Noise Cancellation and Transparency mode in a sweat- and water-resistant design. The earbuds offer up to 30 hours of battery life with the charging case, so you can listen all day.

Shark PowerPro Flex Pet Plus cordless vacuum (18% off)

The Shark PowerPro runs for 50 minutes, enough to tackle your whole home. The brushroll automatically detects the type of flooring, adjusting for the best clean.
